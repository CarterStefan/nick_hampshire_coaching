About Nick Hampshire

"Helping to empower speakers to find their voice and inspire audiences."

Meet Nick

Nick Hampshire is a seasoned coach with almost two decades of experience helping individuals and organisations communicate with confidence and energy. His passion is to help people speak with confidence and inspiring individuals to live with energy

My Mission

My mission is to inspire people to inspire themselves. To give people the confidence and energy to create be who they want to be. Whether you're preparing for a keynote, a business presentation, or your first speech. I believe everyone can become a powerful communicator with the right support and training.

Personal Interests

Outside of coaching, Nick enjoys running, participating in charity work raising thousands for brain tumor research, and sharing inspiration through storytelling.

Nick is a huge believer that movement and conversation are foundational for protecting our physical and mental health. So much so he recently set up 'Running Minds' building a community of people who are doing just that. Showing up and moving their bodies, whilst having purposeful conversations.

Why work with me

My journey has helped hundreds of leaders transform their confidence and communication. I blend mindset, confidence, and energy coaching for lasting leadership impact.

  • Credibility: Almost 20 years coaching leaders and teams
  • Transformation: Clients report breakthrough results in presentations and leadership
  • A unique blend: Mindset, confidence, and energy for sustainable change

For companies

  • Higher energy means more productive teams and reduced burnout

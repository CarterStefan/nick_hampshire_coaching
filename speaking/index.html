Meet Nick on Stage

Nick Hampshire is a communication coach, keynote speaker, and event host who brings energy, warmth, and insight to every stage. With a background in leadership, coaching, and performance, Nick helps audiences connect ideas to action — whether leading a keynote, moderating a panel, or hosting a live event.

Nick’s style combines authenticity and humour with a deep understanding of human communication, leaving audiences inspired and equipped to make real change.

As an event host / MC, I ensure every moment moves smoothly—from setting the tone, transitions between segments, moderating panels, introducing speakers, keeping to timeline, to engaging the audience. Whether it’s a senior leadership conference, a high-profile awards night, or a charity gala, I bring clarity, calm confidence, and warm humour to ensure the event is memorable and seamless.

Professional Event Hosting

From conferences to charity galas, Nick brings structure, humour, and a natural connection with audiences — keeping events flowing smoothly and memorably.

  • Full event facilitation: openers, closers, transitions, introducing speakers, managing surprises
  • Panel moderation & live interviews, with seamless flow and audience-inclusive Q&A
  • Liaising with organisers behind the scenes to maintain timing, adjust on the fly, and troubleshoot
  • Tailored tone: formal, playful, inspiring – aligned to your brand values and event audience
  • Energy control: reading the room, keeping momentum high, ensuring downtime is also purposeful

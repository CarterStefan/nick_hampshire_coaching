How to Overcome Stage Fright

May 2024

Stage fright is a common challenge for speakers at all levels. Here are a few tips to help you overcome it:

  • Practice your speech multiple times.
  • Visualize your success on stage.
  • Focus on your message, not yourself.
  • Take deep breaths to calm your nerves.
  • Remember, the audience is on your side!

With the right preparation and mindset, you can turn anxiety into excitement and deliver a memorable performance.
